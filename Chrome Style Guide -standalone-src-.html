Shrink & scroll behavior

Tab width = min(240, trackWidth / tabCount). As tabs are added each shrinks evenly from the 240px max. At the 52px minimum the label and close button drop out, leaving only the favicon. Below that the strip stops shrinking and becomes horizontally scrollable — the mouse wheel scrolls it left/right. The active tab always keeps its label and close button.
